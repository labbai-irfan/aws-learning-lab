## 🧠 The 60-Second Summary

- **Cloud computing** = renting IT resources (servers, storage, databases) over the internet, on demand, pay-as-you-go — instead of buying and running your own hardware.
- **AWS** (Amazon Web Services) is the largest public cloud provider, offering 200+ services.
- AWS runs in **Regions** (geographic areas) made of **Availability Zones** (clusters of data centers), with **Edge Locations** for fast content delivery worldwide.
- The **Shared Responsibility Model** splits security: AWS secures the cloud *infrastructure*; you secure what you put *in* the cloud.
- You pay only for what you use; the **Free Tier** lets you learn at near-zero cost; **Cost Explorer** and the **Billing Dashboard** keep spending under control.
