# Module 10 — 100 IAM & Security MCQs (with Answers)

> Covers IAM (users, roles, policies, evaluation, STS), KMS, Secrets Manager, encryption, network security, and detective services (CloudTrail, Config, GuardDuty). Attempt first, then check the **Answer Key** at the bottom. Aim for 80%+.
